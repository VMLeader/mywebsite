The 7 dimensions

How you lead. Not how you think you lead.

Each dimension captures a distinct driver of natural behavior. The gap between your natural score and your adapted score is where the insight lives.

DR

Drive + Independence

How much you push, challenge norms, and operate autonomously. High DR leaders move fast, make unilateral calls, and resist oversight. Low DR leaders build consensus and proceed carefully.

SE

Social Energy

How you generate and spend energy through people interaction. High SE leaders are energized by collaboration, relationships, and visibility. Low SE leaders prefer focused, independent work.

PA

Pace + Patience

How fast or measured you naturally operate. High PA leaders are steady, methodical, and prefer proven processes. Low PA leaders drive urgency, change course quickly, and are energized by rapid iteration.

SO

Structure + Order

How much you depend on rules, protocols, and defined process. High SO leaders operate best with clear structure and established guidelines. Low SO leaders excel in ambiguity and resist rigid procedure.

CB

Creative Battery

Your natural orientation toward creative problem-solving, novelty, and idea generation. High CB leaders thrive with open-ended challenges. Low CB leaders prefer proven approaches and execution over invention.

LA

Logic + Analysis

How naturally you operate from data, evidence, and analytical rigor. High LA leaders prioritize objectivity, research, and measured decision-making. Low LA leaders decide from intuition and experience.

VI

Vision + Ideas

How you see possibilities, connect patterns, and think at a systems level. High VI leaders are future-oriented, strategic, and energized by complexity. Low VI leaders stay grounded in what's immediate and actionable.
