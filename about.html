About Vertical Motion

Vertical Motion is a team of former CEOs, military officers, operators, and coaches who've lived the leadership challenges that break companies at scale. We don't teach theory. We work from experience.

Our mission

Upgrade the team. Unlock the next stage.

Vertical Motion was founded to mentor aspiring and emerging executives on how to build their own executive style, executive presence, and executive network.

The ELT Scaling Suite is the product of what we've learned working inside fast-scaling companies, coaching CEOs through transitions, and watching leadership teams succeed and fail in real time.

We believe the most important work a CEO can do at scale is upgrade the team around them. Not add headcount. Not raise more capital. Upgrade the team.

Founder's Story

Tom Cox

Founder • Executive Coach • Author • 3x CEO • Army Veteran • Leukemia Survivor

Tom has closed more than $1.5B in new business throughout his career, has an MBA from Georgia State University, and holds multiple patents for his work in satellite technology innovation. Tom also served in the US Army as a combat medic and satellite controller, and volunteers as a mentor and speaker at Patriot Boot Camp, which helps veterans and military spouses enter the world of entrepreneurship. He lives in Denver, Colorado with his wife of 20+ years and two teenage children.

In August of 2022, Tom was diagnosed with Acute Myeloid Leukemia and given less than two months to live. Six months of multiple hospitalizations, chemotherapy, and a handful of near-death experiences helped Tom to realize that of all the things he has done in his career, inspiring and mentoring others was the most rewarding and energizing for him.

With a second chance at life, Tom is leaning into these skills. Tom started Vertical Motion to mentor aspiring and emerging executives on how to build their own executive style, executive presence and executive network. With 15+ years experience in VP and C-level executive roles in high-growth environments, Tom has the advantage of having sat in the executive chair himself, and is excited to mentor and energize an entirely new generation of business leaders on how to become the best, most authentic leaders they can be.

CM

Chris Martin

Vertical Motion Certified Coach

Chris came to leadership through an unlikely door. He graduated with a fine arts degree; an unexpected opportunity landed him in wealth management, and he never looked back. The fine arts background gave him a different lens than most of his peers, a habit of noticing what others walked past. That perspective never left him. Neither did a core belief: that people are almost always more capable than they know.

Over the next 20 years, Chris worked inside organizations at nearly every scale, from a mid-size wealth management firm to a Fortune 500 brokerage to a high-growth fintech. What he kept seeing, regardless of size, was the same pattern: organizations drift as a natural consequence of growth. The same conversations resurface in slightly different form. Nothing is obviously broken, but progress slows anyway.

That outside perspective is what Chris brings today. He works with founders and emerging leaders who are ready for an honest outside read, someone who can see the system clearly, name what's actually happening, and help them reframe their situation so they can move forward with confidence. Chris holds an MBA from the University of Denver and a Project Management Professional (PMP) certification. He is based in North Vancouver, BC.
